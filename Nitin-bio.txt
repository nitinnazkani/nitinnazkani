Nitin is a re-searcher cum Assistant Professor at Shiv Nadar University Noida, India, where he works on computer vision and machine learning. He received a PhD in Computer Science from IIT Bombay in 2020, where he was advised by Prof. Suyash P. Awate and Ajit Rajwade (Co-guide), and he received ME in Computer Science and Automation from IISc Bengaluru in 2013. His works have received awards at ICIP-2017, MICCAI-2017.
